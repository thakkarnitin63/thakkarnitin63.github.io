Deep Reinforcement Learning for Robot Navigation

  • GitHub URL: Project Link

This project investigates the application of Deep Reinforcement Learning (DRL) to improve autonomous navigation and decision-making in mobile robots within simulated escape rooms. Utilizing advanced algorithms like DDPG and TD3, we demonstrate enhancements in robot adaptability and problem-solving capabilities.

Our results highlight the potential of DRL to transform autonomous robotic systems by developing sophisticated navigational strategies in dynamic and complex environments.

This study emphasizes the crucial role of DRL in advancing robotic technology for applications such as disaster response, search and rescue operations, and automated warehousing, showcasing significant improvements in autonomous operations.
