Deep Reinforcement Learning for Robot Navigation

  • GitHub URL: Project Link

The project uses Deep Reinforcement Learning (DRL) algorithms, specifically DDPG and TD3, to enhance robot navigation in a simulated escape room. It focuses on utilizing kinematic models to navigate and make dynamic decisions efficiently, with the objective to develop autonomous systems capable of operating in complex environments like disaster recovery and automated warehousing.

This initiative underscores the potential of DRL in robotic applications, with expectation of substantial improvements in adaptability and decision-making capabilities of robots under challenging conditions.
